About WARSCAN

Real-time conflict intelligence and evacuation assistance

What is WARSCAN?

WARSCAN is an open-source OSINT dashboard that helps civilians in conflict zones stay informed and safe. It aggregates data from multiple verified news and humanitarian sources, cross-references reports to determine reliability, and provides actionable evacuation routing.

Privacy

Your location data is never persistently stored. Geolocation is requested only when you click "Detect My Location" and is used solely for proximity alerts and route calculation. "I'm Safe" reports are anonymous, token-based, and automatically expire after 24 hours.

Limitations & Disclaimer

  • Data may be delayed, incomplete, or inaccurate
  • Evacuation routes are suggestions, not guarantees of safety
  • Always follow official government and military guidance
  • This tool supplements but does not replace official emergency services
  • Not liable for decisions made based on this information
