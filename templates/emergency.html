First Aid Quick Reference

Severe Bleeding
  1. Apply direct pressure with clean cloth
  2. Don't remove cloth — add more layers
  3. Elevate wound above heart
  4. Tourniquet only as last resort for limbs
CPR (Adult)
  1. Check responsiveness, call for help
  2. Heel of hand on center of chest
  3. 30 compressions, 2" deep, 100–120/min
  4. 2 rescue breaths, repeat until help arrives

Documents

  • Emergency contacts
  • Medical records
